Capstone Project Proposal

Topic & Audience

My topic is about the importance of prioritizing inclusive design for marginalized users.

There are three target audiences: UX Designers, Corporate CEOs, and Project Stakeholders, all of whom have the power to initiate and reform the industry standard of inclusive design.

UX Designers can learn about the best practices and design patterns that contribute towards inclusive design, as well as bolster their empathy for the audiences they are designing for.

Corporate CEOs can reform and invest resources that prioritize design and ensure that designers have a seat at the VP level alongside developers and program managers.

Project Stakeholders can understand the importance and long-term benefits of inclusive design and why it matters just as much as designing for aesthetics.

Game Plan

I plan to work on this project solo.

Lenses

This topic will mostly rely on speculative design, challenging mental models, and data visualization.

Features

Some of the features that I imagine this project will include are a simulation of a poorly designed interface, and an inclusively designed one. The user will experience the juxtaposition between the two to gain a deeper understanding of the importance of inclusive design.

I also plan on having some data visualization about the statistics of visual impairments, and what percentage of the population experience it.

I think it would also be informative to include quotes and anecdotes from individuals with visual impairments.

Tangents

One concern that I have is I am unsure of whether or not to include simulations for disabilities besides visual impairments since it would be difficult to find individuals to interview, and this project has a time constraint.
